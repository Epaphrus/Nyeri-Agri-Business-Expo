Agenda

1. Opening Ceremony: Welcome address by the organizers highlighting the importance of agriculture in the economy and the goals of the expo.

2. Exhibition Booths: Participants showcase their latest agricultural products, technologies, and services. Attendees can explore innovations in farming equipment, seeds, fertilizers, and more.

3. Networking Sessions: Opportunities for attendees to connect with industry experts, farmers, suppliers, and potential business partners. Networking sessions facilitate knowledge exchange and collaboration.

4. Product Demonstrations:Live demonstrations of cutting-edge agricultural technologies and equipment. Attendees can witness the performance and benefits of innovative solutions firsthand.

5. Closing Ceremony: Recap of key highlights from the expo, acknowledgment of sponsors and participants, and closing remarks. The ceremony may also include awards presentation to outstanding exhibitors or individuals contributing to the advancement of agriculture.
