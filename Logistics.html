Logistics

Location

The agri-business expo will be held at a strategically chosen venue with ample space to accommodate exhibitors, attendees, and various activities. The location will be easily accessible by road, preferably situated close to major highways or transport hubs for convenient commuting.

Weather Considerations

We have considered the prevailing weather conditions during the event dates to ensure attendees comfort and safety.

Road Access

The venue has well-maintained road access, with clear signage and directions to guide attendees to the expo site. Parking facilities will be available onsite to accommodate vehicles of exhibitors and attendees, with designated areas for loading and unloading equipment and supplies.

Facilities

Exhibition Halls: Spacious exhibition spaces will be provided to showcase products, technologies, and services offered by participating companies across various sectors of agri-business.

Catering Services: Onsite catering services will offer a variety of refreshments, snacks, and meals to keep attendees energized throughout the event.

Restrooms: Adequate restroom facilities will be available onsite, conveniently located to ensure accessibility for all attendees.

Security and Safety

The expo venue will have appropriate security measures in place to safeguard attendees, exhibitors, and their assets. Trained security personnel will patrol the premises, and emergency response protocols will be established to address any unforeseen incidents promptly.

Information Services

Information desks and signage will be strategically placed throughout the venue to provide assistance, directions, and event schedules to attendees.
