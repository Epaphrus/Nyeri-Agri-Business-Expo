Training Sessions at the Nyeri Agri-Business Expo

~ Animal Health ~

Workshop Title: "Optimizing Livestock Health and Welfare: Best Practices and Innovations"

"Description:" This workshop will cover topics such as disease prevention, vaccination strategies, and the latest advancements in animal health technologies. Experts will share insights on improving animal welfare while maximizing productivity.

1. Genetics and Breeding
Training Session: "Enhancing Livestock Genetics for Improved Performance"

Description: Attendees will learn about modern breeding techniques, genetic selection criteria, and the role of genomics in livestock improvement. Case studies and practical demonstrations will highlight the impact of genetic advancements on farm profitability.

2. Supplement and Feed
Workshop Title: Nutritional Strategies for Livestock: Balancing Health and Productivity"

Description: This workshop will delve into the formulation of balanced diets, the importance of feed quality, and innovative feed additives for optimizing animal nutrition. Participants will gain practical insights into improving feed efficiency and overall herd performance..

3. Dairy Equipment
Training Session: "Maximizing Efficiency with Dairy Equipment and Automation"

Description: Experts will demonstrate the latest dairy equipment technologies, including milking systems, cooling solutions, and automated feeding systems. Attendees will learn how to streamline operations, reduce labour costs, and enhance milk quality through equipment upgrades.

4. Research and Education
Workshop Title: "Research Innovations in Agri-Business: Bridging Science and Practice"

Description: This session will showcase cutting-edge research findings in agriculture, covering topics such as crop improvement, animal genetics, and sustainable farming practices. Participants will gain insights into translating research into practical applications on the farm.

5. Finance
Training Session: "Financial Management for Agri-Business Success"

Description: This session will provide practical guidance on financial planning, budgeting, and risk management for agri-businesses. Experts will discuss strategies for accessing capital, managing cash flow, and optimizing profitability in a dynamic agricultural market

6. Government and Policy
Workshop Title: "Navigating Agricultural Policies and Regulations"

Description: Attendees will learn about key agricultural policies, regulatory frameworks, and government support programs impacting the agri-business sector. Experts will provide insights into compliance requirements, market access considerations, and advocacy strategies.

7. Machinery
Training Session: "Optimizing Machinery Performance for Farm Efficiency"

Description: This session will focus on machinery selection, maintenance best practices, and technological advancements in agricultural equipment. Participants will gain practical knowledge on maximizing machinery uptime, minimizing operating costs, and improving overall farm efficiency These training sessions aim to empower attendees with practical knowledge, skills, and tools to enhance productivity, profitability, and sustainability in their respective areas of agri-business.
